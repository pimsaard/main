Pariwat Imsa-ard

Associate Professor, Faculty of Liberal Arts, Thammasat University

Lecturer, researcher, and academic specializing in English Language Teaching (ELT) and Language Assessment. My work explores the psychology of language learning—examining boredom, anxiety, and curiosity—and leverages quantitative methods like RGT and SEM. Currently advancing the Cognitive Preservation Intelligence (CPI) framework for AI integration in education.

Contact: pariwat@tu.ac.th
