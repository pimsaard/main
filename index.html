Pariwat Imsa-ard

Associate Professor, Faculty of Liberal Arts, Thammasat University

Lecturer, researcher, and enthusiast in language testing and assessment, English language teaching, and the psychology of language learning.

My research focuses on English Language Teaching (ELT), specifically employing quantitative research methods and RGT. I am highly interested in the psychology of language learning (e.g., boredom, anxiety, curiosity) and the development of the Cognitive Preservation Intelligence (CPI) framework for AI integration in language education.

Contact: pariwat@tu.ac.th
